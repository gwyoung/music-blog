About
Named after the fun phrase that a family member once used to describe my music tastes, Bricks Clapping In The Dark is a blog for recommending and writing about music with a focus on overlooked and underrated albums both new and new to me.

This site was created in response to the prevailing negativity in the hype-and-destroy cycle of the music criticism industry these days and as such everything presented here is a glowing recommendation.
